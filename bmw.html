BMW Models

1 Series Sedan

The BMW 1 Series is a front wheel drive 5 door with 5 seats, powered by a 2.0L Turbo 4 Cylinder engine that has 180 kW of power (at 5000 rpm) and 360 Nm of torque (at 1500 rpm) via a 8 Speed Auto Steptronic Sport.

2 Series Sedan

Both 2-series Gran Coupe models come with a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder. The one in the 228i makes 228 horsepower, whereas the M235i's produces 301 horses. All-wheel drive is standard on the M235i and optional on the 228i; an eight-speed automatic transmission is standard across the line.

3 Series Sedan

The 3 series received an exterior and interior redesign for the 2023 model year. Its exterior features new stylings, an updated bumper, and slimmer headlights. As for the interior, a new curved display boasting a 12.3-inch digital gauge cluster and a 14.9-inch infotainment screen is now standard across the lineup.

5 Series Sedan

Premium 2023 BMW 5 Series configurations offer a 335-horsepower turbocharged 3.0-liter 6-cylinder engine that delivers 331 lb-ft of torque while pairing with all-wheel drive. Upgrade to the M5 for a 600-horsepower 4.4-liter TwinPower Turbo V8 engine that generates 553 lb-ft of torque and offers sport brakes.

7 Series Sedan

The 2023 7 Series features everything you've come to expect from a luxury vehicle -automatic 4-zone climate control, multiple power outlets, and extended ambient lighting. The 2023 BMW 7 Series defines comfort and quality with these amenities and many more.
